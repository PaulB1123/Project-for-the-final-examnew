ABOUT ME

Two years later I moved to Denmark and started working for Eric Landon, first as his apprentice and later as an assistant at Tortus Copenhagen.

I graduated (MFA degree) from the Bauhaus University in Weimar, Germany. Before I got my hands dirty with clay, I was working as a graphic designer, mostly in Warsaw, Poland. I discovered ceramics around 10 years ago. After moving to Oslo in 2012 I became a workshop assistant in a shared working space/studio.
